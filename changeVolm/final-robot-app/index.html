Web Audio API Experiments

Messing around with the web audio api:

  • /press-j-until-the-smiths: after a midi version of 'this charming man' loads, pressing j will have it play and randomly draw a shape to the canvas on each frame.
  • /random-pixels: after a midi version of 'girlfriend in a coma loads', pressing j will have it play and randomly draw uniform pixels to the canvas on each frame.
  • /frequency-pixels: after a midi version of '' loads, pressing j will have it play and randomly draw pixels whose shapes are determined by the frequency of the current frame onto the canvas.
  • /high-frequency-erase: after a midi version of 'last night i dreamt that somebody loved me' loads, pressing j will have it erase the white depending on the frequency of the song, revealing a photo of the smiths.
